Some important events in Einstein's life

  • 1879" - Born in Ulma, Germany"
  • 1895" - Fails exams for Zurich's Swiss Federal Polytecnic admission"
  • 1896" - Passes the exam and meets Mileva Marić"
  • 1900" - Awarded the Federal Polytechnic teaching diploma"
  • 1903" - Marries Mileva Marić"
  • 1904" - Birth of Albert's son, Hans Albert Einstein"
  • 1905" - Annus Mirabilis, he publishes four groundbreaking papers including Special Relativity"
  • 1910" - Birth of Eduard, Einstein's second son"
  • 1916" - General Relativity is published"
  • 1919" - Einstein and Mileva obtain a divorce, E. marries his cousin Elsa and Special Relativity is proven during solar eclipse"
  • 1922" - Awarded Nobel Prize"
  • 1933" - Nazis seize power in Germany, Elsa dies in Princeton"
  • 1939"- Signs a letter to President Franklin D. Roosevelt urging the acceleration of atomic bomb development"
  • 1945" - Atomic bomb dropped, end of World War II and Einstein considered the inventor of the bomb"
  • 1955" - Dies in New Jersey"
